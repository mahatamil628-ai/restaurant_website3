Our Story

Artisanal Passion, Organic Roots

Founded in 2012, Savory & Sage was born out of a desire to reconnect fine dining with the changing seasons of nature. We believe that exceptional dining starts long before the kitchen—it begins in the rich soils of our local partner farms.

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Julian Vance, merges classic technique with contemporary innovation to elevate humble ingredients into sensory stories. Every herb, spice, and cut of beef is hand-selected and prepared to celebrate pure, unadulterated flavor.
